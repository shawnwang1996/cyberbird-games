PLANT MERGE: ZOMBIE WAR

Description

In the chaotic world of Plant Merge: Zombie War, arm your garden with strategic merges to fend off relentless zombie hordes!

Master upgrades, deploy special abilities, and outsmart waves of undead foes in this addictive merge-tower defense adventure. Will your botanical army prevail in the ultimate war for survival?

"Dive in and merge your way to victory!"

Instructions

  • • Merge plants to crush zombie waves!
  • • Drag and drop identical plants to evolve them.
  • • Strategically place your plants for maximum defense.
